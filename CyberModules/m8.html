M8: User Experience and Usability

Pre-Class Activity

Select a website or an app where you have an account. Try to reset your password for the website or app without accessing your email. Briefly describe your experience:

  • Which website/app did you choose? Why?
  • What challenges did you face due to not accessing your email in the password reset process? How did you resolve these challenges?

Hands-On Activity

Choose one of the following sites and opt-out of cookies:

  • http://www.ubereats.com
  • https://www.walmart.com
  • https://www.anthem.com
  • https://www.statefarm.com
  • https://www.nike.com

Describe your opt-out experience. Was it difficult or easy to opt-out of cookies? Did you observe any dark patterns? How could the opt-out process be improved for better user experience?
