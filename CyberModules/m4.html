M4: Secure Software Development Lifecycle

Pre-Class Activity

To design a secure system, you need think about those who might be interested in attacking the system:

  • What are possible threats to the system?
  • Who are the adversaries behind these threats?
  • What are the motivations of the adversaries?
  • How likely are they to succeed?
  • Etc.
