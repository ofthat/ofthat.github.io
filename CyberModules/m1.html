Pre-Class Activity

Watch this video by the security expert Caleb Barlow from IBM: Where is Cybercrime Really Coming From?

Questions:

  1. Why do cybercriminals attack?
  2. Do you agree or disagree with the suggestion made by Caleb Barlow at the end of the video? Why or why not?
  3. Why do you think people would fall for cyberattacks, such as the banking phishing attack mentioned in the talk?

Hands-On Activity

The three principles of the CIA Triad are Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability. Consider which of these principles are violated in the following scenario:

A university professor receives a phishing email. The professor does not recognize it as phishing and logs into a fake website using university credentials. The attacker steals the credentials and uses them to log into the university website to gather information about students in the professorâ€™s classes.

  1. Which CIA principles were violated?
  2. What else could the attacker do with the stolen credentials that would violate other CIA principles?
