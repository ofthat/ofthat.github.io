M6: Data Security

Pre-Class Activity

Consider what types of data you will obtain and store for your project. Who would have access to this data? Should they have access?

Video

Outline

  • Data states
  • Full disk encryption
  • Access control
  • Password hashing and salted hashing
  • Password strength
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Social engineering

Hands-On Activity

Determine how you will securely store any passwords that are involved in your project. Will you use multi-factor authentication in addition? If yes, in what form? If no, why not?

Application

  1. Discuss how this module can be applied to your project.
  2. List tasks relevant to your project based on consideration of this module.
