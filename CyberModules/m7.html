M7: Intrusion Detection

Pre-Class Activity

Consider what valuable information you might gain by tracking user behavior and how users interact with the software you are developing. Consider how adversaries might attack your system, and how you would detect the attack.

Video

Outline

  • Anti-virus software
  • Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS)
  • Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS)
  • Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  • Completely Automated Public Turing Test to Tell Computers and Humans Apart (CAPTCHA)
  • Phishing messages
  • Multi-factor authentication

Hands-On Activity

Consider the following questions:

  • How will you protect logs and analytics?
  • What are possible downsides to saving detailed logs and/or collecting extensive analytics? How can you overcome these issues?

Application

  1. Discuss how this module can be applied to your project.
  2. List tasks relevant to your project based on consideration of this module.
